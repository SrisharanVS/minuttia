What Nvidia’s CEO Can Teach Us About Content Creation (Framework)

ByGeorge Chasiotis
July 17, 2024

How do you usually formulate your emails?

We bet most of you don’t shy away from lengthy explanations and details to be as specific about the topic as possible.

But if you send such an email to Jensen Huang, CEO of Nvidia, he will get mad.

At least that’s what former Nvidia employees told Business Insider.

Apparently, Jensen follows a very concise approach to email writing and demands the same from his co-workers.

What is this approach?

We’ve studied it front to back and masterminded a framework you can apply to your content processes, too.

But before we dive into that, we need to explore Nvidia’s organizational structure and how it impacts internal communication.
